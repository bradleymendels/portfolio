About Me

Previous Work


Laities Breeze


2024, clay, wood, hardware


Installed as part of an installation for UNSW, this work sees a bell suspended between two cement pillars. A motor sits in a wooden box on the ground. As the motor slowly rotates, a string connects the box to the bell above, chiming the bell with each rotation.


The striker slowly rotates in its shell, sometimes grinding against its rough clay walls until eventually its friction is broken and it swings out, tapping the side. This process is repeated 3 times a minute. Each reverberation fills the room in an orderly manner. A repetitious knell that is symbolic of something not yet articulated.

Odd Crack Filled


2024, wood, calico, hardware


Odd Crack Filled is a three dimensional work to be viewed in a low light room and permanently installed in the artists bedroom. The main structure of this work has been cut out of Marine ply-wood. Its unusual triangular shape has been made to fit flush behind the open door of the artist’s bedroom. This work draws attention back to the space through its abstract form, prompting the audience to consider unused spaces in their own life and how they might be able to bring beauty to areas that seem to simply take up space.


The illuminated aspect of this work additionally attempts to bring focus and function to this space that is usually devoid of purpose. In this object, its lightbulbs have been moved to the interior, providing the object with a secrecy that the audience is not privy to. Additionally, this light is obscured behind it’s curtains in order to hide the contents of the object. This transforms the object not simply from a place that should now be looked at and seen, but also to a place that contains something important and exclusive. All of these features have been implemented in order to allude to the fact that there is beauty and reverence, especially within the unseen and often cast aside places of our lives.

Outsider 2024


General Merchant


As part of our involvement and contribution to Outsider Festival 2024, General Merchant oversaw the planning and installation of main stage and all surrounding lighting elements. This involved LED setup and programming, Laser rigging and programming, and constant, real-time input throughout the course of the three day festival.

Outsider Festival took place on Darkinjung land, we pay our respect to the first nations community that welcomed us onto their country. Sovereignty was never ceded.
